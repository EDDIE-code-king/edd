DJ EDDIE 254 Rider

Below are the technical and hospitality requirements for a smooth DJ performance at your event.

Technical Requirements

  • 2 x Pioneer CDJ-2000NXS or equivalent (linked via LAN)
  • 1 x Pioneer DJM-900NXS Mixer
  • High-quality sound system appropriate for the venue size
  • 2 x Stage monitors (minimum 12 inches)
  • Power supply with surge protection
  • DJ booth or table with enough space for equipment
  • Lighting system (optional but recommended)
  • High-speed internet (for live streaming, if required)

Hospitality Requirements

  • 1 x Private, secure dressing room with a mirror
  • Bottled water (6 x 500ml)
  • Light snacks or meal
  • Access to clean restrooms
  • Complimentary parking for one vehicle close to the venue

Please ensure all technical and hospitality requirements are met for the best performance experience. If any of these requirements cannot be met, please discuss in advance.
